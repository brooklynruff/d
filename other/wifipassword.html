Steps to use

- Go to chrome://net-export
- Under "OPTIONS" make sure to check "Include raw bytes"
- Click "Start Logging to Disk"
- Go to chrome://policy
- Click "Reload Policies"
- Go back to chrome://net-export and click "Stop Logging"
- Upload file above
